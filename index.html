Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who currently plays as a forward for Manchester United and the Portugal national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time.

About Ronaldo

Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985 in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. He began his football career with Nacional before moving to Sporting CP in 2002. He then signed with Manchester United in 2003 and helped the team win three Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League.

In 2009, Ronaldo transferred to Real Madrid for a then-record fee of £80 million. He spent nine seasons with the club, during which he won four UEFA Champions League titles and two La Liga titles. He also won four Ballon d'Or awards during this time.

In 2018, Ronaldo transferred to Juventus for a fee of €100 million. He spent three seasons with the club, during which he won two Serie A titles. In August 2021, he returned to Manchester United on a two-year contract.
